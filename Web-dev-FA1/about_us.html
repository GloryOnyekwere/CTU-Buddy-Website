About Us

Welcome to CTU-Buddy, a revolutionary platform designed to enhance the educational experience at City Technical University (CTU). Our mission is to provide students with a user-friendly platform that assists them in managing their timetables, engaging in meaningful discussions, sharing valuable resources, and connecting with their peers and educators effectively.

With CTU-Buddy, we aim to foster academic success and create an environment conducive to collaborative learning and personal growth. Our platform is tailored to meet the needs of today's students by integrating essential tools for time management, communication, and resource sharing.
