Overview

CTU-Buddy is a dynamic educational platform designed specifically for City Technical University students. Our goal is to enhance your learning experience by providing all the resources you will need to thrive. From managing your schedule with the Timetable page to engaging in collaborative discussions, sharing valuable resources, and staying connected with peers and educators, CTU-Buddy is your go-to tool for academic success. Whether you're navigating your coursework or seeking support, CTU-Buddy is here to empower you every step of the way.
